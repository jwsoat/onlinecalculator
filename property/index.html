NZ Property Market Basics

New Zealand's property market is unique: no traditional capital gains tax for owner-occupiers, a bright-line test that can tax investment sales within 2 years, LVR restrictions from the Reserve Bank, and KiwiSaver-funded first-home buyer support. These calculators help you understand the financial side before signing anything.
