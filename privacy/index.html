Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What We Collect

OnlineCalculator.co.nz does not require any sign-up or personal information to use any calculator. All calculations happen in your browser — values you enter never leave your device or get sent to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use these external services that may collect data via cookies or similar technologies:

Google AdSense

We display advertising via Google AdSense. AdSense uses cookies to serve personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising via Google Ad Settings.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand which calculators get used and to improve the site. GA4 collects anonymised data about page visits, device type, and approximate location (city level).

Google Fonts

We load DM Sans and DM Mono fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log IP addresses when fonts are requested.

Cookies

Third-party cookies from Google AdSense and Analytics are used as described above. We don't set any first-party cookies of our own. You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Your Rights (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

Under the NZ Privacy Act 2020, you have rights to access and correct personal information held about you. Since we don't collect personal information directly, requests should be made to the third-party services listed above (Google).

Privacy questions: privacy@onlinecalculator.co.nz
