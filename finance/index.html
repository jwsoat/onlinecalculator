About NZ Finance Calculations

New Zealand has unique financial rules — 15% GST since 2010, progressive tax brackets revised in 2024, KiwiSaver with employer and government top-ups, and the bright-line test on residential property. Every calculator here is built specifically for New Zealand rates, thresholds, and rules. Always confirm with IRD (ird.govt.nz) or a qualified accountant for tax filing.
