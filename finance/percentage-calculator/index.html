Percentage Formulas

X% of Y: Result = X ÷ 100 × Y
X is what % of Y: Result = X ÷ Y × 100
% change from A to B: Result = (B − A) ÷ A × 100

Common Uses

Discounts: 25% off $80 = $20 off → pay $60. Use "% of X" mode.

Tips and tax: 15% GST on $200 = $30 GST → total $230. (For NZ GST specifically use our GST Calculator.)

Salary increases: Salary went from $65k to $72k → 10.8% pay rise. Use "% Change" mode.

FAQ

What's the difference between % change and percentage points? ▾
If interest rates move from 5% to 7%, that's a 2 percentage point increase but a 40% relative change. They're often confused in headlines.
