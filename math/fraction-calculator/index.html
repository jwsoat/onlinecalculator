Fraction Formulas

Add: a/b + c/d = (ad + bc) / bd
Subtract: a/b − c/d = (ad − bc) / bd
Multiply: a/b × c/d = ac / bd
Divide: a/b ÷ c/d = ad / bc

The result is always simplified by dividing numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (GCD).

FAQ

What is a mixed number? ▾
A whole number combined with a fraction, e.g. 1 ½. Improper fractions like 3/2 can be written as 1 ½ (the whole part) and the fractional remainder.
