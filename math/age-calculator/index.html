How Age Is Calculated

This calculator counts complete years, then the leftover months, then the leftover days. It accounts for varying month lengths (28–31 days) and leap years correctly.

FAQ

Does this account for leap years? ▾
Yes. The calculation uses native JavaScript Date objects, which handle leap years (1 day added to February every 4 years, except century years not divisible by 400).
Can I find time between any two dates? ▾
Yes. The "Date of Birth" can be any date in the past, and "As Of" can be any future or past date.
