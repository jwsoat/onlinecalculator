About OnlineCalculator.co.nz

OnlineCalculator.co.nz is a free, New Zealand-specific calculator hub. We build and maintain practical tools for everyday Kiwi financial, property, health, and building calculations — the kind of numbers you actually need to look up, not generic international estimates that ignore NZ tax rules, NZ building code, or NZ property law.

What we build and why

Every calculator on this site is built from scratch for New Zealand. The GST calculator uses exactly 15% because that is the NZ rate — not 10% (Australia) or 20% (UK). The PAYE calculator uses the current Inland Revenue tax brackets and the ACC earner levy rate confirmed for the 2025–26 tax year. The mortgage calculator offers weekly and fortnightly repayment options because that is how most NZ banks structure home loans. Generic international calculators get these details wrong, and wrong numbers cost people money.

We cover five categories: Finance & Tax (GST, PAYE, KiwiSaver, mortgages, loans, compound interest, RUC), Property & Rental (rental yield, LVR, bright-line test, rent vs buy, real estate commission), Health & Fitness (BMI, calories, pregnancy due date, ideal weight), Construction & Home (concrete, paint, decking, retaining walls), and Maths & General (scientific, fractions, age, NCEA grades).

How we keep tools accurate

Each tool is reviewed whenever the underlying rules change — IRD rate updates, RBNZ LVR announcements, ACC levy changes, or NZ Building Code updates. We check primary government sources directly rather than relying on third-party summaries. When a rate changes we update the calculator logic, update the on-page explanatory text, and update the lastmod date in our sitemap so search engines know to re-crawl.

Calculations run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No inputs are sent to any server. There is no account system, no tracking of what numbers you enter, and no cookies beyond what our ad network sets (see our Privacy Policy).

Our sources

The following authoritative New Zealand sources inform our calculators and explanatory content:

  • Finance & Tax — Inland Revenue (IRD) for PAYE tax brackets, ACC earner levy, KiwiSaver contribution rates, and GST.
  • Property — Reserve Bank of New Zealand (RBNZ) for LVR restrictions and OCR; IRD Bright-Line guidance for the property sales tax rules.
  • Health — NZ Ministry of Health for BMI classifications; Mifflin-St Jeor equation for calorie/TDEE calculations; standard obstetric formula (Naegele's rule) for pregnancy due date.
  • Construction — Ministry of Business, Innovation & Employment (MBIE) — Building for NZ Building Code requirements; standard NZ timber treatment specifications (H3.2/H4) from NZS 3602.
  • Road User Charges — Waka Kotahi NZTA for current RUC rates by vehicle class.

Who we are

We have lived and worked in New Zealand, which means we understand why it matters that a mortgage calculator defaults to fortnightly repayments, why the bright-line test has specific date rules, and why construction calculators need to reference H-treated timber grades.
