NZ Building Tips

Always order 5–10% extra to allow for waste, offcuts, and breakage. NZ Building Code requires consents for structural work, plumbing, and most decks over 1.5 m above ground. Check with your local council before starting. Use H3.2 or higher treated timber for outdoor decks, fence posts, and bearers.
